How it works

4 steps to your departure

A clear and structured journey designed to move you forward with confidence. Click each step to learn more.

Everything begins with a comprehensive one-on-one consultation. We take the time to listen and understand your academic background, personal goals, budget, and timeline. This initial meeting is free and without any commitment.

  • Review of your academic records and level of education
  • Identification of the best country and program for your profile
  • Honest assessment of your eligibility and chances of admission
  • Presentation of the full procedure, fees, and timeline
  • Answer to all your questions before you decide

Once the destination is chosen, our team prepares and submits your complete application file to our partner university. We handle all communication with the institution on your behalf.

  • Collection and verification of all required documents
  • Translation and legalization of documents when needed
  • Official submission to the partner university
  • Active follow-up until the admission letter is received
  • Payment of admission fees and enrollment confirmation

With your admission letter in hand, we build a solid and complete visa file. We prepare you thoroughly for your embassy appointment to maximize your chances of success.

  • Complete assembly of the visa file according to embassy requirements
  • Preparation and coaching for the embassy interview
  • Medical exam scheduling and support (where required)
  • Booking of the embassy appointment
  • Follow-up on the visa application status until the decision

Once your visa is obtained, we handle the final preparations for your departure and ensure your first days abroad go smoothly. You won't face this chapter alone.

  • Assistance with flight booking at the best available rates
  • Housing arrangement in your destination country
  • Airport transfer coordination upon arrival
  • Guidance for university registration on arrival
  • Ongoing support and follow-up throughout your studies

Required documents

Documents required for all destinations, with country-specific details. Our team guides you through every requirement.

📄 Basic documents (all countries)

  • ✓
    High school diploma (BAC)
  • ✓
    BAC transcripts (all years)
  • ✓
    High school report cards
  • ✓
    Valid passport (min. 2 years validity)
  • ✓
    Passport-size ID photos
  • ✓
    Birth certificate extract
For Master's degree programs: Add undergraduate transcripts and Bachelor's diploma.

🔎 Country-specific requirements

  • 🇨🇳
    China: Criminal record + bank statement also required
  • 🇺🇸
    USA: Bank statement (6 months) + sponsor's ID
  • 🇨🇦
    Canada: Guarantor with sufficient financial proof
  • 🇹🇷
    Turkey: deposit required at temporary admission
  • 🇮🇳
    India: deposit required at temporary admission
  • 🇪🇸
    Spain: Proof of financial resources + health insurance
  • 🇧🇪
    Belgium: NARIC credential recognition + proof of accommodation
