Enrollment & Admission
Your first step toward a university abroad
← Back to all services

Getting into a foreign university can seem overwhelming — different systems, different languages, different requirements. Our team handles the entire admission process on your behalf, from choosing the right institution to receiving your official acceptance letter.

What we do for you

  • Analysis of your academic profile and identification of the best-fit universities
  • Preparation and verification of all required documents
  • Translation and legalization of documents when needed
  • Official submission of your application to our partner universities
  • Active follow-up with the institution until your admission letter is received
  • Guidance on choosing the right program and specialization

Partner universities

  • Canada: Collège de l'Outaouais, Collège La Cité (Gatineau & Ottawa)
  • USA: Seattle Colleges (Central, North, South)
  • Malaysia: APU — Asia Pacific University, INTI International University
  • Turkey: Universities in Antalya and Istanbul
  • North Cyprus: Final International University
  • Tunisia: IAE Tunis
  • India, China, Russia, Spain, Belgium: selected partner institutions

Why it matters

  • Avoid costly mistakes on your application that could lead to rejection
  • Benefit from our direct relationships with universities for faster processing
  • Receive honest feedback on your eligibility before committing
  • Save weeks of back-and-forth with institutions abroad
